how Mods work

Your Mod journey in 4 simple steps

01

Create your first Mod

If you want to start a journey in our Mods world, the first step is to create your own Mod, which is totally yours. After the Mod is created, it is ready to be sent on your profile.

02

Sell Mods

After creating Mod and uploading it to your profile, now it's possible for others to buy your Mod. If someone buys your Mod, money will be sent to your balance.

03

Get licenses for popular brands

A license will give you a bigger opportunity to make similar Mods to reality, which is very popular and you will have a higher chance of selling your mods.

04

Having partnership with game owner

After doing all steps, your hard work must be sent to the owner of the game — the person who gives you partnership and offers you a job which is well-paid and challenging.
